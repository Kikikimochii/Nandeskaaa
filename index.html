📦 Everyone brings
🔥 No cooking allowed — all food must be ready to eat. No stoves, no portable grills.
🔢
3-6-9 BIPO
Elimination · play as many rounds as you want!
📖
Count up from 1 in turns. When your number contains the digit 3, 6 or 9 — shout BIPO! instead of saying it.
✅
Contains = any digit, not multiples!
3→BIPO · 13→BIPO · 30→BIPO · 33→BIPO BIPO (two 3s = two BIPOs!) · 63→BIPO BIPO · But 12, 15, 22... → say normally.
💀
Wrong number, missing BIPO, wrong BIPO count → eliminated! Count restarts from 1.
🏆
Points per round:
1st out = 0 pts · 2nd out = 1 pt · 3rd out = 2 pts · Last standing = 3 pts
🔁
Play multiple rounds! After each round, lock in the points and play again. Scores stack up.
🎨
Relay Drawing
4 rounds per session · 1 secret spy among the drawers!
📱
Setup: Google "Pictionary word generator" for a random word. Each round: 1 guesser, 3 drawers (1 spy hidden among them).
🕵️
Before drawing, shuffle 3 papers face-down — 2 say "Drawer", 1 says "Spy". Each drawer picks one secretly. Don't show anyone!
▶️
Guesser looks away. 1st drawer draws the word → passes to 2nd who draws what they see → 3rd does the same. Show guesser all 3 drawings.
🤫
Spy's mission: draw just bad enough to confuse the guesser — but act innocent! Too obvious = get caught 😂
🤔
Guesser has 30 seconds to guess. Correct → guesser +1 pt!
👆
After the round, everyone argues — who is the spy? If spy escapes → tap their name in the scoreboard → spy +1 pt! Caught = no point, no tap.
🔄
4 rounds per session: R1→KC guesses · R2→XL · R3→ZX · R4→WN. Lock in and play another session!
📊 Overall scoring
Total = all BIPO rounds + all Drawing pts (guesser ✅ + spy 🕵️)
🏆 Highest total → Reward (KC decides!)
😈 Lowest total → Penalty (everyone decides!)
⚡ Tie → sudden death BIPO
